Midnight Shift

By the blue hush of the monitor, the cat kept watch like a tiny sovereign in a fur-lined throne. Its paws rested near the keys, but it seemed to be listening to the machine more than using it. The room held the soft electric smell of lateness, and every blinking cursor felt like a firefly trapped in glass.
