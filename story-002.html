At the edge of the room, Jill sat upright before the glowing screen, her paws poised with a seriousness that made the keyboard seem like a tiny piano. The office light pooled softly over her fur, and the monitor painted her whiskers in pale blue. She watched the cursor blink as if it were a heartbeat waiting for her command.
